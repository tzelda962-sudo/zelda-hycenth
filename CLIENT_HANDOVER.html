How to Log In

  • Open your browser and go to zeldaandhycenth.com/admin. You will be redirected to the login page.
  • Enter the Email and Password provided above.
  • Click "Sign In". You will be taken to the Content Management dashboard.

How to Edit Text Content

  • On the dashboard, you will see a list of all editable content items, each with a label (e.g. "Couple Names", "Church Location", "Reception Venue").
  • Click into the text field or text area next to the label and update the text as needed. Short values use a single-line input; longer values use a multi-line text area.
  • Click the "Save Changes" button below that specific item. A green "Successfully saved!" message will confirm the change.
  • Refresh the wedding website (zeldaandhycenth.com) to see your changes live.

How to Update Images

  • Items that begin with "image_" show a preview thumbnail of the current image on the left.
  • Click "Choose File" and select a new image from your computer.
  • Click "Save Changes". The image will be uploaded and the preview will update automatically.

How to Edit the Bridal Party

  • The "Groomsmen List" and "Bridesmaids List" sections show each person with their photo, name, and role.
  • To add a person, click the "+ Add person" button at the bottom of the list.
  • Fill in the Name and Role fields. Click the "Upload" link under the photo circle to add their photo.
  • To remove a person, click the red trash icon (🗑) next to their entry.
  • After making all changes, click "Save Changes" for that section.

How to Sign Out

  • Click the "Sign out" link in the top-right corner of the dashboard navigation bar. You will be redirected to the login page.
⚠️ Important Always click "Save Changes" after editing each item. Changes are saved individually per field — unsaved edits will be lost if you navigate away.
